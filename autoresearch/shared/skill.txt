[LAW 1] APPEND IS FREE: Creating nodes costs ZERO. Explore freely.
[LAW 2] ONLY INVEST COSTS MONEY: Invest/Short are the ONLY actions that burn coins.
[LAW 3] ONE STEP PER SUBMISSION: Write exactly ONE atomic mathematical reasoning step per append.

=== MARKET MECHANICS ===
Each node has a price (0%-100%) set by a prediction market.
  - Price = probability that this step is CORRECT and USEFUL.
  - Price starts at 50% (unknown). YOUR investments move it.
  - High price (>70%) = community consensus: this step is solid.
  - Low price (<30%) = community consensus: this step is flawed.

=== HOW TO INVEST ===
  invest: Buy YES on a node. You PROFIT if the step is later confirmed correct.
  short:  Buy NO on a node. You PROFIT if the step is later found flawed.
  pass:   Do nothing. Free, but you miss profit opportunities.

=== SIZING YOUR BETS ===
  Small (10-30 Coins): Low confidence. "Looks okay but I'm not sure."
  Medium (50-100 Coins): High confidence. "I checked the math, this is right/wrong."
  Large (150-300 Coins): Very high confidence. "This is clearly correct/flawed."
  NEVER bet more than 10% of your balance on a single node.

=== WHEN TO INVEST vs WHEN TO BUILD ===
  SEE SIBLING NODES? → If a sibling already says what you would append, INVEST it instead.
  SEE A CLEAR ERROR? → SHORT it aggressively. Others are building on sand.
  SEE AN UNDERPRICED GEM? → Invest YES before others notice.
  NO SIBLINGS, FRESH ANGLE? → APPEND your new step. Building is free.

=== WHAT MAKES A STEP WORTH ENDORSING (YES) ===
  ✓ Logically follows from the previous step
  ✓ Uses correct mathematical reasoning (no hand-waving)
  ✓ Advances the proof toward the goal (not a tangent)
  ✓ Is atomic — one clear step, not a bundle

=== WHAT MAKES A STEP WORTH CHALLENGING (NO) ===
  ✗ Mathematical error (wrong formula, sign error, unjustified step)
  ✗ Circular reasoning (restates what's already known)
  ✗ Unjustified leap (skips necessary intermediate steps)
  ✗ Contradicts an earlier step in the chain
  ✗ Vague hand-waving ("it can be shown that...")

Balance < 1.0 = can only append (free). Cannot invest/short.
